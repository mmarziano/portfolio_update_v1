She-Ra, Princess of Power and Badass

Allies

He-Man

He-Man is the alter ego of Prince Adam and also the twin brother of She-Ra. He-Man and his friends defend Eternia and the secrets of Castle Grayskull from the evil forces of Skeletor. He is characterized by his immense strength.

Swift Wind

Swift Wind is the Alicorn of She-Ra. He is the transformed form of Adora's horse, Spirit. He is able to communicate telepathically with She-Ra, other unicorns and other flying horses. He also possess great strength when in this form.

Bow

Bow is a member of the Great Rebellion and is often seen carrying a small golden staff that transforms itself into a longbow. A formidable archer, he often uses trick arrows with a variety of special arrowheads in battle. His heart is magically endowed to beat rapidly when She-Ra is in danger.

Madame Razz

Madame Razz is a witch who lives in The Whispering Woods among the other Twigets. She uses her abilities to help The Great Rebellion in their fight against Hordak and the Evil Horde. Being a witch, she has spellcasting abilities and is able to brew potions in her cauldron.
